**NUEVA SITUACIÓN:** Mujer de tez blanca/clara ante los estantes de un supermercado premium, comparando precios en tiempo real.

Her mouth is slightly compressed, jaw tightened. This is the private moment when someone realizes that inflation isn't a news headline — it's the disappearance of purchasing power in real time.

Her posture is upright but weighted — the body language of someone who understands something unsettling about her own reality. Her left arm rests at her side, gripping a shopping basket lightly.

The key detail: the price tags are clearly visible in soft focus beside her finger — the numbers are legible enough to read but blurred enough that the viewer understands "this is the moment of realization," not data analysis.

On the shelf: identical or similar products, but with visible price differences. A promotional banner or price label visible but not dominating. The supermarket's clean, well-lit shelving is prominent.

Lighting: Bright, harsh supermarket lighting from overhead (the institutional, cool fluorescent kind) combined with soft natural light from nearby windows. The overhead light creates slight shadows under her cheekbones, emphasizing the moment of concern. The cool, bright quality of supermarket lighting is intentional — it mirrors the clinical reality of the moment.
